Real life stuff

Things that matter more than people think

  • Having a backup payment card if one gets flagged.
  • Keeping your meds, phone charger, and passport in your carry-on.
  • Knowing your daily bank withdrawal limit before you need cash.
  • Packing for heat, humidity, and constant walking instead of overpacking layers.
Course ready

Bring what helps you create, not just what looks cool

  • Laptop or tablet for coursework and editing.
  • Phone tripod, portable charger, and storage space for content.
  • Apps downloaded before departure so you are ready on day one.
  • Comfortable day bag for filming, note-taking, and daily travel.
