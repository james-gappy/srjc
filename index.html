What students should do before departure

  • Check passport validity and make backup copies
  • Confirm phone plan, data access, and apps
  • Review bank card fees and notify the bank
  • Pack thoughtfully for daily walking and coursework
  • Bring academic tools for content creation and reflection
Designed for students: This site is a practical guide for first-time and returning travelers. Use it to get organized early and avoid common travel issues before departure.
